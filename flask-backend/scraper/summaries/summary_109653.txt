Here's a summary of the City of London Community and Protective Services Committee meeting for residents:

*   **Initiated** discussion on a proposed by-law change that would require landlords to obtain a license before asking tenants to leave for renovations. Residents can provide feedback at a public meeting on July 15, 2024.
*   **Approved** a grant agreement with Youth Opportunities Unlimited to continue "Project Clean Slate," a program that helps young people in the community.
*   **Directed** city staff to submit the annual update of the "Housing Stability for All Plan" to the province, outlining the city's strategies for preventing homelessness and improving housing stability.
*   **Directed** staff to review the recent CPKC train fire, learn from the incident, and propose improvements to how residents are notified during emergencies.
*   **Received** an update report from the Accessibility Community Advisory Committee, highlighting ongoing efforts to make the city more accessible for all residents.
